Stacy Davidson

Tenure: Stacy Davidson has been a stylist since 2003, in her 10 years she has trained in Italy, New York, Minnesota, Las Vegas, Los Angeles and San Monica. She has advanced schooling in at least nine different color and products lines, but specializes in Aveda Color and Product! Her creativity in styling hair and designing "that special look" has been showcased in fashion shows, hair shows, photo shoots, and magazines! Stacy has trained with Aveda Academy, Vidal Sassoon, and Bumble and Bumble.

Specialties:

  • Long Layers
  • Short haircuts (bobs)
  • Updos
  • Extensions
  • Weddings
  • Redheads
